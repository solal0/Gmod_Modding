Why do i need to go through the verification gate ?
It's a way to fund/support me without you spending a single cent, you go through few ads, i get some money and you're granted access to my scripts for 24 hours.
How does the verification gate work ?
There's 3 checkpoints, click verify, follow the steps, wait for validation and get redirected to the website, do the same process 3 times to unlock all 3 checkpoints. Once completed, on https://solal0.github.io/Gmod_Modding/ click continue, input your SteamID64 in the textbox and click verify. If the id is valid you'll be added for the next 24 hours to the access granted list. Then in Gmod, after executing one of my scripts, execute shs_verify in the console to verify yourself. If the script finds your SteamID64 in the list you'll get access to my script.
How do i get my SteamID64 ?
I recommend using https://steamid.xyz/, input your steam profile url and he'll get all the type of ids including SteamID64. Tho you can use anything else that works, just make sure it's the correct SteamID64 and not someone elses.
